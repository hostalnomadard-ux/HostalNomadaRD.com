# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Budget-conscious boutique travelers visiting Santiago de los Caballeros, RD — backpackers, digital nomads, domestic tourists, and cultural travelers who want authentic local immersion, not a resort. Spanish and English speakers. They value character, history, and a social atmosphere over luxury amenities. They find the hostal via Airbnb, Hostelworld, and organic search.

## Product Purpose

Convert site visitors into bookings. The website is the primary brand touchpoint outside Airbnb/Hostelworld listings. It should communicate the unique character of an 1948 Art Déco building in the historic center of Santiago — the history, the Cervecería Búcaro, the personal hosts — and make booking feel natural and obvious. Success = a visitor understands what makes Nómada different and clicks "Reservar."

## Brand Personality

Histórico · Cálido · Social. A place with soul: the building has a story, the hosts are present, and the brewery downstairs means it's alive. Approachable and warm without being casual-cheap. Boutique sensibility without luxury pretension.

## Anti-references

- **Big-chain hotels (Marriott, Hilton)**: No corporate polish, sterile stock photography, or "luxury destination" positioning. This is not a premium hotel.
- **Over-curated premium boutiques**: Avoid hyper-styled minimalism that feels exclusive or intimidating to the budget-boutique traveler. No "too picky, square, or premium lux" vibes.
- **Generic hostel sites**: No cheap-party-hostel clichés — neon, scattered photos, zero personality. Nómada is above that.
- **Dominican resort/tourism sites**: No Punta Cana beach-resort aesthetic, no "Visit Dominican Republic" tourism stock imagery.

## Design Principles

1. **Lead with the building.** The 1948 Art Déco building is the brand's single strongest differentiator. Every page should make it feel present.
2. **Warmth over polish.** Approachable language and warm visuals beat corporate-clean. The hosts live here; the design should feel like it too.
3. **Show, don't describe.** Real photos, real reviews, real history — not adjectives. "Art Déco de 1948" is more powerful than "unique historic stay."
4. **Social without noise.** The Cervecería Búcaro is a key draw and differentiator. Feature it clearly without it overwhelming the lodging offer.
5. **Friction-free booking.** Every page should have a clear path to reservations. Don't make the traveler hunt for how to book.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Bilingual audience (Spanish primary, English secondary). No stated WCAG level target, but WCAG AA as baseline. Users include older travelers — avoid small touch targets. Content must be legible in bright sunlight (mobile in outdoor contexts).
